About Me

I am a native New Yorker with a diverse background in technology, media, fashion, hospitality and design. My career started in the adversting and marketing industry, working at publications such as Rolling Stone, Vibe, and Surface. After a few years, I shifted to hospitality management, where I was given the opportunity to run some of the most successful restaurants and hotels in the country.

Recently, I have been doing project management at a leading hardware/software company. It's here where I developed a true passion for technology, particulary in web development and design. I enjoy creating projects for others, incorporating my past experiences to deliver a customized final product. When I'm not working you can usually find me in hiking upstate, cooking, or obsessing over my matchbook collection.
